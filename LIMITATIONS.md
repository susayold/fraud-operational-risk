# Limitations

- The observed benchmark contains only two days of PCA-anonymized transactions from 2013.
- PCA features do not support normal business reason codes, customer segmentation or merchant interpretation.
- Operational controls, capacity, incidents and friendly-fraud workflows are synthetic.
- Benchmark performance does not represent current production fraud performance.
- No AML, sanctions, identity, device consortium, chargeback or live platform data is available.
- Fairness review is an operational proxy review, not a legal conclusion.
- Author validation is not independent model validation or production approval.
